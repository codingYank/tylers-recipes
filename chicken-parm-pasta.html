Chicken Parmesan Pasta

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • 2 - 3 Boneless Skinless Chicken Breasts (dices into bite sized pieces)
  • 1 tsp Italian Seasoning
  • 1/2 tsp Garlic Powder
  • 1 Minced Medium Yellow Onion
  • 3 Minced Garlic Cloves
  • 1 lb Box Short Pasta
  • 24oz Jar marinara Sauce
  • 1 cup Mozzarella Cheese
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan Cheese
  • Salt and Pepper

Instructions

  • In a large pot or skillet, add a drizzle of olive oil and heat over medium-high. Add the chicken and season with salt, pepper, italian seasoning, and garlic powder. Cook for around five minutes until chicken is cooked. remove chicken to a plate
  • Add onion and garlic to the pot and cook for 2 - 3 minutes until soft. Pour in the sauce, fill empty sauce jar with water and add to the pot. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a strong simmer
  • Add chicken and pasta to the pot, stir, and cover and cook until pasta is done.
  • Stir in parmesan and 1/4 cup mozzarella
  • Sprinkle remaining cheese on top and cook for 2 - 3 minutes, until cheese is melted. Serve
