Roasted Red Potatoes

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lbs Red Potatoes (diced)
  • 1/4 cup Olive Oil
  • 2 tsp Minced Garlic
  • 1 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 Pepper
  • 1 tsp Italian Seasoning
  • 1/4 cup Finely Grated Parmesean

Instructions

  • Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Line baking sheet with foil and spray with cooking spray
  • Place potatoes in a large bowl. Add olive oil, garlic, salt, pepper, Italian seasoning, and parmesean. Toss to coat
  • Spead potatoes in a single layer on baking sheet. Bake for 35 - 45 minutes or until golden and tender.
