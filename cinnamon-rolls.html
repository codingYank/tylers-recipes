Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients (For Dough)

  • 1 QT Whole Milk
  • 1 cup Vegetable Oil
  • 1 cup Sugar
  • 2 Packages Yeast
  • 9 cups All Purpose Flour (1 cup reserved)
  • 1 tsp heaping Baking Powder
  • 1 tsp Baking Soda
  • 1 tbls heaping Salt

Ingredients (For Filling)

  • 1/2 cup Melted Butter
  • 1 cup Sugar
  • 1 cup Brown Sugar
  • 6 tbls Cinnamon
  • 1 tsp Salt

Instructions

  • Heat milk, oil, and 1 cup of sugar in a large pot until just under boil, stirring occasionally
  • Continue to stir occasionally until around 110 degrees. Add yeast
  • After 1 minute add 8 cups of flour and stir until just combined
  • Cover with damp kitchen towel and let sit in a warm place for 1 hour
  • Add baking powder, baking soda, salt, and remaining flour. Stir thouroughly, careful not to tear a lot of the gluten strands
  • Let the dough rest for 15 minutes
  • Preheat oven to 365 degrees
  • Place half the dough on a large, floured work area
  • Roll dough into large (30" X 10") rectangle
  • Spread half on the melted butter over the rectagle (leave about half an inch along one of the long side dry)
  • Sprinkle half of the cinnamon sugar mixture over the buttered area
  • Roll the dough and seal along the unbuttered strip
  • Cut the rolled dough into 12 equal parts
  • Place rolls in a buttered pan (I use 9 X 11 foil pans, 6 in each)
  • repeat with the other half of the dough and the rest of the butter and cinnamon and sugar
  • Cover the pans with damp kitchen towel and let rise for 30 minutes
  • Remove towel and bake for 15 - 18 minutes, should be golden brown and not too mushy in the middle

Cream Cheese Icing

Ingredients

  • 6 oz Softened Cream Cheese
  • 1/3 cup Softened Butter
  • 2 cups Powdered Sugar
  • 1/2 tbls Vanilla
  • Pinch Salt

Instructions

  • Cream butter and cream cheese with mixer
  • Gradually add the sugar
  • Add salt and vanilla
  • Spread over rolls while still warm

Maple Coffee Icing

Ingredients

  • 1 bag Powdered Sugar
  • 2 tsp Maple Flavoring
  • 1/2 cup Milk
  • 1/4 cup Melted Butter
  • 1/4 cup Strong Brewed Coffee
  • Pinch Salt

Instructions

  • Cream butter and 1 cup of sugar with mixer
  • Alternate adding milk, sugar, coffee
  • Add maple flavoring and salt
  • Spread over rolls while still warm
