Ham and Cheese Pasta

Ingredients

  • 2 tbls Butter
  • 1/2 large Yellow Onion, sliced
  • Salt Pepper
  • 1 lb Diced Cooked Ham
  • 1 lb box Bow Tie Pasta
  • 4 cloves Minced Garlic
  • 1 tbls Dijon Mustard
  • 1/2 cup White Wine
  • 4 1/2 cup Water
  • 8 oz block Jarlsberg Cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded Parmesan

Instructions

  • Melt butter over medium heat in a large pot
  • Once melted, add onion, salt, and pepper. Cook until onion are soft (5 - 7 minutes)
  • Add ham, pasta, garlic, mustard, wine, water, and more salt
  • Bring to a simmer and cook for 9 - 13 minutes, stirring regularly, intil pasta is done
  • Remove from heat and add in cheese
