Creamy Mediterranean Chicken Skillet

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Boneless Skinless Chicken Breast (sliced into cutlets)
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 2 tbls Butter
  • 1 Diced Onion
  • 4 Minced Garlic Cloves
  • 12 oz Finely Diced Roasted Red Peppers
  • 2 1/2 cups Chicken Stock
  • 7 oz Cream Cheese (herb/garlic best)
  • 2 cups Baby Spinich
  • 2 cup Cooked Pasta

Instructions

  • In a large skillet, melt the butter
  • Season the chicken and cook on medium high heat until browned and heated through. Put chicken on a plate and keep warm
  • In the skillet cook the onions until soft, then add garlic and peppers
  • Season with salt and pepper
  • Cook for 2 minutes then add cream cheese and stock
  • Whisk the stock until blended, simmer for around 7 minutes
  • Add the spinich and toss into the sauce until wilted
  • Add the chicken back to the skillet and allow to warm through
  • Plate the pasta, add chicken and spoon sauce on top
