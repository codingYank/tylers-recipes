Boneless Lamb Loin

Ingredients

  • 1 Boneless Lamb Loin (about 2 pounds, split lengthwise)
  • 2 1/2 tbls Paprika
  • Salt
  • 2 tbls Garlic Powder
  • 1 tbls Black Pepper
  • 1 tbls Onion Powder
  • 1 tbls Cayenne Pepper
  • 1 tbls Dried Oregano
  • 1 tbls Dried Thyme
  • 1/4 cup Olive Oil
  • 2 sprigs Fresh Rosemary
  • 1/2 tsp Ground Coriander
  • 2 tsp Ground Cumin

Instructions

  • Combine 2 tbls salt, garlic powder, black pepper, onion powder, cayenne pepper, dried oregano, and dried thyme. Set aside seasoning
  • Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  • On a large cutting surface, lay out lamb cut side up and open. Sprinkle inside with salt and seasoning and place a rosemary sprig inside. Roll up lamb and tie with other rosemary sprig. Season outside with salt, coriander, and cumin
  • In a large skillet over high heat, add olive oil and heat until hot, Add lamb and sear on all sides until golden careful not to burn the rosemary
  • Put lamb in oven and cook for another 20 minutes, or until meduim rare. Remove from oven and allow to rest for 10 minutes
  • Slice into 1 inch pieces and serve
