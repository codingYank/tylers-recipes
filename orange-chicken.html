Orange Chicken

Ingredients

  • 24 oz bag Popcorn Chicken
  • 1 cup Orange Juice
  • 1/2 cup Sugar
  • 2 tbls Rice Vinegar
  • 2 tbls Reduced Sodium Soy Sauce
  • 1/4 tsp Ginger
  • 1/4 tsp Garlic Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Red Chili Flakes
  • Orange Zest from half an orange
  • 1 tbls Cornstarch

Instructions

  • In medium pot, add orange juice, sugar, vinegar, soy sauce, ginger, garlic, and red chili flakes. Bring to a boil.
  • In a small bowl, whisk cornstarch with 2 tablespoons of water. Add to orange sauce and whisk together.
  • Continue to cook until suace has thickened. Once thickened reduce heat to low and add orange zest
  • Fry popcorn chicken until heated through. Drain and add to large metal bowl
  • Pour sauce over chicken and mix. Serve immediately. Goes great with Lo Mein.
