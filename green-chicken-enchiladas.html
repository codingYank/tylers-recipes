Green Chicken Enchiladas

Ingredients

  • 4 cups Cooked Shredded Chicken
  • 2 tbsp Taco Seasoning
  • 3 cups Shredded Mozzarella
  • 3 cups Shredded Monterey Jack
  • 1 cup Sour Cream
  • 10 10" tortillas (Corn preffered)
  • 28oz Green Enchilada Sauce

Instructions

  • Preheat oven to 375
  • In a large bowl mix the chicken, 2 cups of mozzarella, 2 cups of Monterey jack, sour cream, and taco seasoning.
  • Wrap tortillas in a damp towl and microwave for 1 minute
  • In a skillet warm the enchilada sauce. Take a tortilla, dip it in the sauce until lightly coated. Scoop 1/3 cup of the filling into the tortilla and roll tightly. Place seam side down in a greased 9X13 baking dish. Repeat with remaining totillas
  • Pour remaining sauce over the enchiladas and top with the remaining cheese
  • Bake until cheese is bubbling (around 25 minutes)
