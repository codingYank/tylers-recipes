Oven-Roasted Asparagus

Ingredients

  • 1 bunch thin Asparagus Spears
  • 3 tbls Olive Oil
  • 1 1/2 tbls grated Parmesan
  • 1 clove Minced Garlic
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper

Instructions

  • Preheat oven to 425 degrees
  • Toss asparagus with oil, then sprinkle with parmesan, garlic, salt, and pepper. Arrange onto greased baking sheet in a single layer
  • Bake until just tender, about 10 minutes
