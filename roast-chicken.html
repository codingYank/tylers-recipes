Garlic Herb Butter Roast Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 4-5 lb Whole Chicken
  • 1/4 cup Unsalted Butter, melted
  • 3 tbls Olive Oil
  • 1/4 cup White Wine
  • 1 Lemon, Halved
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 2 tbls Parsley
  • 4 Cloves Minced Garlic
  • 1 Head Garlic, peeled and cut in half horizontally
  • 3 Fresh Rosemary Sprigs
  • Butchers Twine

Instructions

  • Preheat oven to 430 degrees
  • Discard neck and remove any excessive fat or leftover feathers. Rinse the chicken with cold water and pat dry
  • Pour olive oil, melted butter, wine, and juice of halt a lemon over bird and under the skin. Season with salt and pepper
  • Rub the minced garlic over the chicken and under the skin
  • Stuff the cavity with garlic head, squeezed lemon half, and the rosemary sprigs. Tie legs together with butchers twine
  • Place breast side up in a roasting pan. Roasting for around 1 hour and 15 minutes until cooked through. Baste every 20 - 30 minutes
  • Remove from oven and cover, allow to rest for 10 minutes. Serve with pan juices and remaining half lemon
