Honey Garlic Chicken

Ingredients

  • 2 Chicken Breasts (about 1 lb total)
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 1/4 cup Flour
  • 3.5 tbsp Butter
  • 2 Minced Garlic Cloves
  • 1.5 tbsp Apple Cider Vinegar
  • 1 tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 1/3 cup Honey

Instructions

  • Cut chicken breasts horizontally to create 4 thin steaks total. Season with salt and pepper
  • Place flour in shallow dish. Coat chicken in flour and shake off excess
  • Melt most of the butter in a large skillet over high heat
  • Place the chicken in the skillet and cook until golden and cooked through
  • Turn down heat to medium high and add remaining butter and garlic
  • Add vinegar, soy sauce, and honey. Combine
  • Allow to simmer until sauce has thickened
  • Coat chicken in sauce and serve
