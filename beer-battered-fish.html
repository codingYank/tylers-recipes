Beer Battered Fish

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lbs Fresh Cod, Haddock or Halibut cut into 4 pieces
  • 1 cup All Purpose Flour
  • 1 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 1/4 cup Very Cold Light Beer
  • Extra Flour for Dredging
  • Oil for Frying

Instructions

  • Heat oil in deep fryer to 350 degrees
  • Combine flour, baking powder, amd salt in a large flat bowl. Pour in the beer and whisk until smooth. In another large flat bowl add flour for dredging
  • Pat fish dry with paper towl. Thoroughly dredge all sides in the flour. Shake of excess
  • Dip the fish in the batter to coat all sides, allowing some of the batter to drip off
  • Fry the fish for 5 - 8 minutes or until nicely golden
