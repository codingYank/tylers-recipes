Pizza Dough

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Hot Water (120 degrees)
  • 1 package Yeast
  • 2 cups Flour (plus extra)
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1 tbls Sugar
  • 1 tbls Olive Oil

Instructions

  • Dissolve yeast in water
  • Add 2 cups flour, salt, and sugar. Mix
  • Flour a work area and knead the dough for 5 minutes or until smooth adding more flour as needed.
  • Place dough in an oiled bowl and top with olive oil
  • Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for 24 hours
