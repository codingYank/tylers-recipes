Lo Mein

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Linguine or Fettaccine
  • 1/3 cup Reduced Sodium Soy Sauce
  • 1/6 cup Teriyaki Sauce
  • 1/2 cup Brown Sugar
  • 1 tsp Garlic Powder
  • 1 tbls Minced Garlic
  • 1-2 tsp Fresh Grated Ginger
  • 4 tbls Seasame Oil

Instructions

  • Cook pasta according for package instructions to al dente
  • Combine soy sauce, Teriyaki sauce, brown sugar, and garlic powder in a small saucepan and bring to a boil. Set aside.
  • In a large skillet, heat sesame oil. Once hot, add minced garlic and ginger, stirring frequently.
  • Once the garlic and ginger are fragrant, add noodles and sauce mixture. Cook until most of the sauce has reduced. Serve immediately.
