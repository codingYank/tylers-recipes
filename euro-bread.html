European-style Crusty Bread

Ingredients

  • 4 1/2 - 5 cups Bread Flour
  • 1 tbls sugar
  • 2 1/4 tsp Instant Yeast
  • 2 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 2/3 cups Lukewarm (90 - 110 degrees F) Water
  • Yellow Cornmeal (for coating pan)

Instructions

  • Stir all ingredients (exept cornmeal) in a large bowl starting with 4 1/2 cups flour
  • Flour a work surface with the remaining 1/2 cup flour and knead the dough for 6 minutes
  • Place the dough in a lightly greased bowl and cover with plastic wrap. Let dough rise until doubled in size (1 - 2 hours)
  • Gently deflate the dough and cut it in half. Pat each half into a 6" x 8" oval
  • Fold each piece like a letter. Gently pat and roll the dough into a log about 10 inches long. Repeat with other piece
  • Sprinkle a pan with the cornmeal and place the loaves seam-side down on the pan
  • Let the dough rise, lightly coved with oiled plastic wrap, for 45 minutes
  • Preheat oven to 450 degrees F and place pan of water on the bottom rack
  • Dust the tops of each loaf with flour and cut 3 or 4 1/2 inch deep diagonal slashes in each loaf
  • Bake for 20 - 25 minutes until golden brown and the bread sounds hollow
  • Turn off the oven and crack the door open, and allow the bread to remain in the oven for another 5 minutes
