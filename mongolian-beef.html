Mongolian Beef

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lbs thin sliced Sirlion Steak
  • 3 tbls Cornstarch
  • 2 tbls Vegetable oil, divided
  • 3 cloves minced Garlic
  • 1 tbls minced Ginger
  • 1/3 cup Soy Sauce
  • 1/3 cup Water
  • 1/2 cup Brown Sugar
  • Salt and Pepper

Instructions

  • Season steak with salt and pepper. Toss in cornstarch until fully coated
  • In a skillet over medium-high heat, add 1 tbls oil garlic and ginger, saute for 1 minute. Add soy sauce, water, and sugar and bring to a boil until sugar is dissolved
  • Pour sauce into a liquid measuring cup and set aside
  • Place skillet back over heat and add remaining oil. Add steak and sear until evenly browned on both sides.
  • Pour sauce back into skillet and toss together with the meat. Allow sauce to thicken for 1 - 2 minutes
  • Serve with Lo Mein.
